This policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal data through the App, and outlines your rights regarding that data.

By using Wenak, you acknowledge that you have read this policy and agree to its terms. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

## 2. Data We Collect

We collect only the following categories of data, limited to what is necessary to provide the Wenak service:

### a) Location Data
- **Foreground location:** while you are actively using the App.
- **Background location:** even when the App is closed or not actively in use, to enable real-time location sharing with your group members — the **core functionality** the App is built around. We only request this permission after showing a clear in-app explanation of its purpose, before the system permission dialog appears.
- Location-related data: accuracy, speed, heading, activity type (stationary/moving), battery level, and charging status.

### b) Device and Notification Identifiers
- Your device's app installation identifier (Firebase Installation ID), used as a stable device key.
- Your push-notification token (FCM Token), used to deliver notifications.
- Your device's operating system and language settings.

### c) Account Data (via Google Sign-In)
- Your display name and email address associated with your Google account.
- Your profile picture (from your Google account at signup, or a photo you upload later).
- **Public Code:** an automatically generated 8-character code unique to each user, used solely for discovery purposes to invite other users to a group — not as an authentication or authorization mechanism.

We **do not** collect phone numbers as a sign-in method, and we **do not** collect or store any payment card details (these are handled entirely through Google Play Billing, as described below).

## 3. How We Use Your Data

We use the data described above exclusively for the following purposes:
- Enabling real-time location sharing between members of the groups you choose to join.
- Sending notifications related to your account and groups (invitations, role changes, etc.).
- Verifying your identity and binding your device to your account (to prevent simultaneous sign-in from multiple devices and protect your data).
- Providing technical support and responding to your inquiries.
- Improving the App's performance and stability.

**We do not use your data for advertising or third-party marketing purposes, and we never sell it under any circumstances.**

---

## 4. Who We Share Your Data With

### a) Your Group Members
- Your **real-time location and activity status** (active/inactive) are visible only to members of groups you voluntarily join, and only while you have location sharing enabled — which you can toggle off at any time, per group.
- **If the owner/admin of a group you belong to is subscribed to our paid (Premium) plan**, they may access your location history within that specific group for up to the **preceding 30 days**. This is an extension, in time, of the same sharing scope you already consented to by joining the group. This access is **strictly limited to your own group members and does not constitute sharing or selling your data to any party outside the group.**

### b) Service Providers (Data Processors)
We use trusted technical service providers to operate the App, contractually bound to process your data only for the stated purpose:
- **Supabase** — database hosting, authentication, and storage.
- **Firebase (Google)** — push notifications (FCM) only. Firebase is not used for authentication or data storage.
- **Google Maps** — in-app map display.
- **Google Play Billing** — subscription payment processing. We never see or store your payment card details; these are managed entirely by Google.

### c) Legal Authorities
We may disclose limited data if legally required to do so by a binding order issued by a competent authority under Egyptian law.

**We do not sell your data to any third party for commercial or advertising purposes under any circumstances.**

## 5. Data Retention

- **Detailed location history:** retained for a **maximum of 30 days**, after which it is automatically and permanently deleted from our systems.
- **Last known location** (used to show your group members your live status): continuously overwritten by your most recent location point; it is not a historical record.
- **Account and profile data:** retained as long as your account is active, and permanently deleted upon account deletion (see Section 7).
- **Notification records:** kept as an internal audit log, and deleted upon account deletion.

---

## 6. Data Security

We apply reasonable security measures to protect your data, including but not limited to:
- Strict database-level access-control policies (Row Level Security), ensuring users can only access data they are explicitly authorized to see.
- No direct client-side database writes; every operation passes through secured server-side functions.
- Encrypted communication between the App and our servers.

**In the event of a security breach affecting your personal data**, we will notify you within **three (3) business days** of becoming aware of the incident, in compliance with Egypt's Personal Data Protection Law.

That said, no electronic system is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee that our systems are immune to unauthorized access, despite taking reasonable available precautions.

## 7. Your Rights

As the data subject, you have the right to:
- **Access** the personal data we hold about you.
- **Correct or update** your data (such as your display name and profile picture) directly within the App.
- **Delete your account entirely**, which results in the permanent deletion of your location, its history, your memberships, and your personal data from our systems (except where limited retention is required to comply with a legal obligation).
- **Withdraw your consent** to location sharing at any time, either by disabling sharing per group or by deleting your account entirely.
- **Contact us** with any question regarding your data at wenakhq@gmail.com.

## 8. Location Accuracy and Service Limitations

We make reasonable efforts to provide accurate, up-to-date location data. However, the nature of background location technology on mobile devices imposes some inherent limitations you should be aware of:

- Location data is **approximate, not meter-precise**, and is transmitted at intervals (not continuously) to preserve your device's battery life.
- If your internet connection is interrupted, location points will be sent retroactively once connectivity resumes; in the meantime, your group members will see your last known location.
- Some operating systems (and certain device manufacturers) impose background-app restrictions to save power, which may delay or temporarily pause your location updates until the App is reopened.
- Force-closing the App immediately stops location sharing.

These limitations exist in any comparable app that relies on background location technology on Android and iOS, and are not defects unique to Wenak. We recommend against relying on Wenak as a critical or emergency safety tool given these inherent technical constraints.
